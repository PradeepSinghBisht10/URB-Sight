Welcome to Smart City

Building a better future for all citizens

Vision Of Smart Cities Mission

The objective of SCM is to promote cities that provide core infrastructure and give a decent quality of life to its citizens, a clean and sustainable environment through the application of 'Smart' solutions.

Gallary

Our Missions....

Sustainable Urban Development

Promote eco-friendly solutions like renewable energy, green buildings, and efficient waste management.

Reduce carbon footprint and increase green spaces.

Efficient Public Transportation & Mobility

Develop smart transport systems (electric buses, metro, bike-sharing, etc.).

Improve traffic management using AI and IoT sensors.

Digital Governance & E-Governance

Provide online services for citizens (bill payments, grievance redressal, etc.).

Ensure transparency and real-time data monitoring.

Water & Waste Management

Implement smart water meters and leakage detection systems.

Promote waste segregation and recycling initiatives.
